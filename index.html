Bio

Before joining UIC, I was a Postdoctoral Fellow working with Makoto Yokoo in Graduate School of Information Science and Electrical Engineering at Kyushu University, and with Hau Chan in Department of Computer Science \& Engineering at University of Nebraska-Lincoln.

I received my PhD in Operations Research from Academy of Mathematics and Systems Science, Chinese Academy of Sciences, under the supervision of Xiaodong Hu and Xujin Chen, and a joint PhD in Computer Science from Department of Computer Science at City University of Hong Kong, under the supervision of Minming Li. I completed my B.S. in Applied Maths at Hua Loo-Keng Class, Beihang University.

Openings

We regularly have several positions for Phd/MPhi students in Computer Science, and Research Assistant (Visiting Scholar) each year.

Research Interests

I am broadly interested in algorithms, AI and computational economics, including problems related to
  • Algorithmic Game Theory and Mechanism Design
  • Computational Social Choice
  • Multi-agent Systems
  • Approximation and Online Algorithms
